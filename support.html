Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use iCare without logging in?

Yes. However, you won't have access to your data usage or custom sounds

How does iCare take measures to protect my data?

No. App & Timer usage is only viewable to the user and the individual data is deleted weekly.

How is iCare different than other smart timers?

We stand out from our competitors by offering extensive functiionality such as allowing users to upload custom notification sounds, view their timer & app usage, and even block the timer from running when certain apps are opened.
