Simply start the timer

iCare prevents eye strain by following the 20-20-20 rule

When the timer ends, users are notified to look at something 20 feet away for a total of 20 seconds

Track app & timer usage
Choose apps to block the timer from running.

If you login, you can view your timer usage and even your individual app usage. Data tracking can be disabled in the settings menu.

You wouldn't want a full-screen overlay if you're in an important meeting or playing an online game. Thus, you can even add apps to a list of blockers that will prevent the timer from running.

Upload custom notification sounds.

When the timer ends, a sound notification can be enabled in settings. As a guest, you can choose from a preset list of custom sounds. If you login, you are able upload your own sounds.
